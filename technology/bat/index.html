Basic Attention Token

Basic Attention Token radically improves the efficiency of digital advertising by creating a new token that can be exchanged between publishers, advertisers and users. It all happens on the Ethereum blockchain.

The token can be used to obtain a variety of advertising and attention-based services on the Brave platform. The utility of the token is based on user attention, which simply means a personâ€™s focused mental engagement.

Digital advertising is broken. It is a market filled with middlemen and fraudsters, hurting users, publishers and advertisers. The Basic Attention Token (BAT) was developed to address this. BAT, an ERC20 token built on top of Ethereum, will be the token of utility in a new, decentralized, open source and efficient blockchain-based digital advertising platform.

The Team

Our motto at Cindercloud is: "Invest in the team, not the product". When it comes to the team over at BAT, we're in for a treat, names such as Brendan Eich (yes, the Brendan Eich), Yan Zhu, Brian Brown, Marshall Rose and many more. Just look at their about page and you'll quickly discover that BAT probably just has about the best people in the field one could find at the moment.

Investment Advise

Pre-ICO, we definitely would've recommended to invest in this ICO. Although there was a lot of noise coming from the community concerning the price they put on their coin offering, as there was quite some volatility on the eth-usd pair. Nevertheless, the team quickly communicated that they don't intend to keep any fundings that could possibly come from such volatility.

For us, this blogpost means a lot about the professionality of the people at BAT. They just want to reach their goal so they can keep on developing BAT.

At this point, the market cap of BAT is more than tenfold of what people initially invested. BAT is definitely a product we're going to follow closely.
